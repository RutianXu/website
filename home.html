Solving the Rubik's Cube involves a variety of methods.
The CFOP method focuses on breaking down the solution into four
key steps: Cross, F2L, OLL, and PLL, known for its speed and efficiency.
The Roux method emphasizes block-building techniques for smoother
solving. Meanwhile, beginners often start with the Beginner's method,
focusing on layers. Whether you're after speed or simplicity, these
methods offer diverse approaches to conquering the cube.
