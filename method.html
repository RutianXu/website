Method

Begginer's Method

Examine the cube's face and locate an edge piece that matches the center color.
For example, if the center is white, find an edge with white.
Position the edge piece by rotating the right face (R) clockwise, then turning
the upper face (U) clockwise, and finally rotating the front face (F) clockwise.
Repeat this process for all four edges to create a cross on the chosen face.
