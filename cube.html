Gan

Gan is a prominent name in the realm of twisty puzzles, particularly
known for its cutting-edge advancements in speedcubing. Among its
standout creations, the Gan 356 X stands as a celebrated product.
What sets Gan apart is its commitment to customization,
allowing users to fine-tune their cubes to perfection.
With features like adjustable magnets and the Gan Elasticity
System (GES) for tension control, these cubes offer speedcubers
a level of precision and adaptability that's unmatched.
This dedication to innovation has solidified Gan's
reputation as a go-to brand for those seeking to achieve
faster solving times and a superior cubing experience.

Moyu

Moyu is a prominent and respected brand in the world of twisty
puzzles and speedcubing, known for producing high-quality cubes
that cater to both beginners and advanced cubers. One of their most
renowned products is the Moyu Weilong GTS3. Moyu's cubes often feature
smooth and stable turning, advanced corner cutting, and a range
of options to suit various solving preferences. Their commitment
to innovation has earned them a solid reputation among cubing enthusiasts,
making Moyu cubes a popular choice for those seeking excellent
performance and reliable quality in their puzzle-solving endeavors.
